Organizations

Latinx Leadership Council (LLC)

The purpose of the Latinx Leadership Council is to unify Latinx organizations by assisting one another in fundraising, professional events and educational well being. We work together to build a stronger sense of unity within the Latinx community of CSUS; all while supporting the student body in pursuing higher education while learning to give back to their communities and further enhance a network of support after graduation.

Website
Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE)

SHPE changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support & development. SHPE's vision is a world where Hispanics are highly valued and influential as leading innovators, scientists, mathematicians and engineers.

Website
Mujeres Ayudando la Raza (MAR)

Our purpose is to create a support group for Chicanx/Latinx students. Our goals are to serve the local community, Chicanx/Latinx community, aid in retention of our members in universities and advocate for cultural and social awareness and pride within the Chicanx/Latinx community.

Brown Issues

Brown Issues is a statewide grassroots organization that cultivates the leaders of tomorrow through civic engagement, narrative change, and social emotional health support. In our Sacramento State Chapter, we focus on creating space for our members to learn and grow in their leadership and advocacy.

Puente Club

Puente Club's mission is to support students in their transition from Highschool and Community College Puente Project to a 4-year university. Any Students who joins the Puente Club will become a Puentista, and as a close-knit "familia" (family), we will help one another reach our common goal of graduating from CSUS.

Salsa Loca

We are a non-profit club/organization on campus with the goal to provide a safe welcoming environment where students and community members can relieve stress, anxiety, and improve mental health and overall wellness through the expression of dance of Latin Music. We want to help inspire people to feel comfortable with themselves and improve their confidence, while teaching them about Latin cultures.

Ballet Folklorico

The purpose of this organization is to exert students to embrace the different types of regional dance within the Mexican culture. We encourage students to explore what this club has to offer to enrich their view of Mexican culture.

Association of Latino Professsionals For America (ALPFA)

ALPFA is designed to empower and develop minorities as leaders of character for the nation in every sector of the American and global market. Our mission is to continue growing our network, expand our relationships with a diversity of professional organizations and develop Latino professionals.

Website
Lead, Engage, and Achieve with Determination Club (LEAD)

Our goal is to engage, motivate, and unite undocumented youth AB540 students & allies at Sacramento State

Spanish Club (Club de Español)

El propósito de esta organización es crear un espacio en el que los estudiantes puedan desarrollar sus ideas y poner en uso su potencial académico. Los diferentes eventos que se llevan a cabo ayudan a los estudiantes a adquirir experiencia profesional que en futuro puede ser empleada en el campo laboral. Además, el club es un espacio en el que los estudiantes pueden llevar a cabo distintos eventos sociales.
